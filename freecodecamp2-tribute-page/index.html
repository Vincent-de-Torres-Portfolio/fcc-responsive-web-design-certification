T'Pol was born in 2088 in the city of Shi'Kahr on Vulcan. She was the only child of T'Les, a Vulcan ambassador, and her husband. (ENT: "Home", "Awakening").

T'Pol was trained in the Vulcan Science Directorate. She was a graduate of the Vulcan Science Academy and was a member of the Vulcan Diplomatic Corps. (ENT: "Broken Bow", "The Seventh", "Home") She was also a member of the Vulcan Ministry of Security. (ENT: "The Seventh") T'Pol was a sub-commander in the Vulcan High Command. (ENT: "The Expanse", "Home")

She was assigned to Enterprise NX-01 in 2151 as an observer from the Vulcan High Command. (ENT: "Broken Bow")

She was assigned to Enterprise as a liaison officer in 2152. (ENT: "The Expanse")

She was assigned to Enterprise as first officer in 2154. (ENT: "Home")

She was assigned to Enterprise as a science officer in 2155. (ENT: "These Are the Voyages...")
